Rules & Controls

Complete horizontal weft lines. Trigger motif bursts when newly locked knots connect to older board knots to form 3+ matching cells diagonally or orthogonally; a plain fresh piece alone will not burst.

  • Move: Arrows or A/D
  • Rotate: Up/W, counter Z
  • Soft drop: Down/S
  • Hard drop: Space
  • Hold: C
  • Use charm: X
  • Pause: P

Charms: Needle clears a column, Knot locks a wild piece, Gale shifts the loom left or right.

Comet Loom

Thread constellations fall through a vertical loom. Stack mixed-color pieces, complete weft lines, connect new knots to older matching knots for motif bursts, and spend charms before tension reaches 100.
